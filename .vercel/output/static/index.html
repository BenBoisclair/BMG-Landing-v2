The Dream Crafting Process

1 Concept
1 Concept & Consultation

If you have an existing model or reference, we perform high-precision 3D scanning. If not, we begin with a design consultation to develop the concept together.

2 3D
2 Prototype Creation

Based on the approved direction, we create a physical prototype using clay or 3D printing. Refinements are made until the form and details meet expectations.

3 CNC
3 CNC Carving

The approved prototype is digitized and optimized for CNC carving. Advanced CNC machines carve the sculpture with precision, following the finalized 3D Design.

4 Hand
4 Hand Detailing & Finishing

Skilled artisans refine every surface by hand to achieve depth, realism, and premium craftsmanship.

5 Delivery
5 Delivery & Installation

The completed work is carefully packaged, transported, and installed on site. Our team ensures precise placement and safety in the final presentation.
