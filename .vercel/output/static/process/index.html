The Dream Crafting Process

Step 01

Initial Idea

Concept or Existing Model

If you have an existing model or reference, we perform high-precision 3D scanning. If not, we begin with a design consultation to develop the concept together.

  • Collaborative design consultation with our expert team
  • High-precision 3D scanning of existing models or references
  • Concept development and initial sketches
  • Review and approval of design direction
  • Material and budget discussion
Step 02

Prototype Creation

Building the Foundation

Based on the approved direction, we create a clay or 3D printing model. Adjustments are made until the design meets expectations.

  • Clay modeling or 3D printing based on initial concept
  • Multiple iterations and refinements
  • Scale model creation for approval
  • Detailed review sessions with client feedback
  • Final prototype approval before production
Step 03

CNC Carving

Advanced Manufacturing

Advanced CNC machines carve the sculpture with precision, faithfully following the finalized 3D data for accurate reproduction.

  • State-of-the-art CNC machinery operation
  • Multi-axis precision carving
  • Continuous quality monitoring during production
  • Stone selection and preparation
  • Initial roughing and detailed finishing passes
Step 04

Hand Detailing & Finishing

Artisan Craftsmanship

Skilled artisans refine every surface by hand to achieve depth, realism, and premium craftsmanship that machines cannot replicate.

  • Expert hand carving and refinement
  • Surface texture enhancement
  • Detail work on intricate features
  • Polishing and finishing treatments
  • Final quality inspection and approval
Step 05

Delivery

Final Transport

The finished piece is carefully packaged using specialized crating and shipped to the customer's specified location worldwide.

  • Custom crating and protective packaging
  • Climate-controlled storage if needed
  • International shipping coordination
  • Real-time tracking and updates
  • Insurance and documentation handling
Step 06

Installation

Handover & Support

Our team can assist with on-site installation, ensuring proper placement and providing ongoing support for your sculpture.

  • Professional on-site installation available
  • Placement guidance and recommendations
  • Care and maintenance instructions
  • Warranty documentation and support
  • Long-term relationship and future projects
